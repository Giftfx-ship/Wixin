Frequently Asked Questions

How do I send money?
Go to Dashboard > Send. Enter recipient email/account, amount, then verify with PIN and 3 BBC codes from admin.
What are BBC codes?
BBC codes are 3-layer security codes required for all transactions. Contact admin@primeheritage.com to get them.
How do I purchase a virtual card?
Go to Cards section, select your card type, and complete purchase with 3 BBC codes.
